** #41 Typer: ASAP delete before reassignment causes use-after-free
  ~x = x.concat(y)~ generated ~Type_delete(x)~ before the assignment,
  but ~concat~ still needed to read ~x~. Fixed by checking
  ~expr_uses_var(rhs, vname)~ — when true, the typer sets
  ~save_old_delete~ on the NODE_ASSIGN and the builder emits:
  ~{ Type *_old = x; x = RHS; Type_delete(_old, ...); }~
