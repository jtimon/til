** #145 Cloning in link directive extraction
  Resolved indirectly. til.til's ~extract_imports~ (til.til:85) and the
  link/link_c extraction loop (til.til:327) are now read-only -- they
  only read directive paths without modifying the AST. The directives
  themselves are stripped by the typer: ~is_compile_directive~
  (typer.til:2352) identifies link/link_c/import FCalls, and the
  desugar passes skip them via ~continue~ so they're naturally dropped
  from ~new_ch~ when ~body.children = new_ch~ runs. The original
  clone-and-swap pattern in til.til no longer exists; the cost of
  cloning kept children moved to the typer and is tracked under [[./144.org][#144]]
  and #146.
